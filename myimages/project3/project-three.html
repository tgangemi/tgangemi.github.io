Bee Happy Candles

Bee Happy Candles is a brand concept I created for a candle brand that promotes self love, environmentally friendly material, and overall mindfulness. This company would also use natural beeswax and eco-friendly/reusable packaging for this product. I created a brand identity from the logo mark I designed, worked with the package design mockup, as well as designed business cards and apparel for the brand.
